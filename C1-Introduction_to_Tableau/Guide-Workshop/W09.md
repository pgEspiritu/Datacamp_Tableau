# 🎨 Hands-On Activity: Make Your Data Visually Appealing in Tableau

---

# 🎯 Learning Objectives

After completing this activity, participants should be able to:

- Apply formatting best practices in Tableau
- Improve readability of charts and dashboards
- Customize titles, labels, and legends
- Use colors effectively for storytelling
- Create dual-axis charts
- Build dashboards and simple story flows
- Evaluate visualization quality using best-practice checklists

---

# 📁 Dataset

Create an Excel file named **VideoGame_Sales_Format.xlsx** using the following data:

| Year | NA Sales | EU Sales | Asia Sales | Other Sales | Total Sales |
|------|---------:|---------:|------------:|------------:|------------:|
| 2005 | 120 | 80 | 60 | 30 | 290 |
| 2006 | 150 | 90 | 70 | 40 | 350 |
| 2007 | 180 | 110 | 85 | 45 | 420 |
| 2008 | 220 | 130 | 95 | 50 | 495 |
| 2009 | 210 | 125 | 90 | 48 | 473 |
| 2010 | 200 | 120 | 88 | 46 | 454 |
| 2011 | 170 | 105 | 80 | 42 | 397 |
| 2012 | 160 | 100 | 75 | 40 | 375 |

---

# Exercise 1: Build a Basic Visualization 📊

## Business Question

> How have video game sales changed over time?

### Steps

1. Open Tableau Public.
2. Connect to **VideoGame_Sales_Format.xlsx**.
3. Drag **Year** to Columns.
4. Drag **Total Sales** to Rows.
5. Change chart type to **Line Chart**.

### Expected Output

A simple line chart showing sales trends.

---

# Exercise 2: Improve Chart Title 📝

## Task

Make the visualization more descriptive.

### Steps

1. Double-click the title.
2. Rename it to:

```text
Global Video Game Sales Trend (2005–2012)
```

### Discussion

Why are descriptive titles important?

---

# Exercise 3: Add Regional Breakdown 📊

## Business Question

> How do different regions contribute to total sales?

### Steps

1. Drag **NA Sales**, **EU Sales**, **Asia Sales**, **Other Sales** to Rows.
2. Change to **Bar Chart**.
3. Use **Color** to differentiate regions.

### Expected Output

A multi-series bar chart.

---

# Exercise 4: Improve Colors 🎨

## Task

Make regions visually distinct.

### Steps

1. Click **Color → Edit Colors**.
2. Assign distinct colors for each region:
   - NA → Blue
   - EU → Green
   - Asia → Red
   - Other → Orange

### Discussion

How do colors improve readability?

---

# Exercise 5: Add Axis Labels 📏

## Task

Ensure clarity in axes.

### Steps

1. Click each axis.
2. Add labels:
   - X-axis: "Year"
   - Y-axis: "Sales (Millions)"

### Expected Output

Clearly labeled axes.

---

# Exercise 6: Create Dual-Axis Chart 📈📉

## Business Question

> How does total sales compare to regional contributions?

### Steps

1. Drag **Total Sales** to Rows.
2. Drag **NA Sales** to Rows (next to Total Sales).
3. Right-click second axis → **Dual Axis**.
4. Synchronize axes.

### Expected Output

Two overlapping charts.

---

# Exercise 7: Customize Tooltips 💬

## Task

Improve interactivity.

### Steps

1. Click **Tooltip** in Marks card.
2. Edit to:

```text
Year: <Year>
Total Sales: <Total Sales>
NA Sales: <NA Sales>
EU Sales: <EU Sales>
Asia Sales: <Asia Sales>
Other Sales: <Other Sales>
```

### Discussion

How do tooltips improve user experience?
