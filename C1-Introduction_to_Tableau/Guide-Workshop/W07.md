# 📅 Hands-On Activity: Working with Dates in Tableau

---

# 🎯 Learning Objectives

After completing this activity, participants should be able to:

- Understand how Tableau handles date fields
- Use date hierarchies (Year, Quarter, Month, Day)
- Drill down and roll up time-based data
- Create time-series visualizations
- Identify seasonal patterns in data
- Compare values across time periods
- Answer business questions using date analysis

# Exercise 1: Understanding Date Fields 📅

## Task

Load **Gapminder_Births_Health.xlsx** into Tableau.

### Steps

1. Open Tableau Public.
2. Connect to the dataset.
3. Go to a new worksheet.
4. Locate:
   - Year
   - Month

### Observation

Check if Tableau assigns a:

📅 Date icon

---

### Discussion

Why does Tableau treat dates as dimensions?

---

# Exercise 2: Create a Time Series Chart 📈

## Business Question

> How do births change over time in Portugal?

### Steps

1. Drag **Month** to Columns.
2. Drag **Births** to Rows.
3. Ensure Month is treated as a **continuous or discrete date field**.
4. Sort months in correct order (Jan → Dec).

### Expected Output

A line chart showing monthly birth trends.

---

# Exercise 3: Apply Date Hierarchy 🌳

## Task

Use Tableau's built-in date hierarchy.

### Steps

1. Drag **Year** to Columns.
2. Click the expand icon (▶).
3. Drill down:

```text
Year → Quarter → Month
```

### Expected Output

A hierarchical time-based visualization.

---

### Discussion

What is the advantage of using date hierarchies?

---

# Exercise 4: Compare Countries Over Time 🌍

## Business Question

> How do Portugal and Chile compare in monthly births?

### Steps

1. Drag **Month** to Columns.
2. Drag **Births** to Rows.
3. Drag **Country** to Color.
4. Change chart type to **Line Chart**.

### Expected Output

Two lines representing Portugal and Chile.

---

# Exercise 5: Identify Seasonality 🔍

## Business Question

> Is there a seasonal pattern in births?

### Steps

1. Create a line chart for Chile.
2. Drag:
   - Month → Columns
   - Births → Rows
3. Sort months correctly.

### Expected Output

A seasonal pattern visualization.

---

### Interpretation

- Which months show higher births?
- Which months show lower births?

# Exercise 6: Highlight Minimum Month 📉

## Business Question

> Which month has the lowest births in Chile?

### Steps

1. Create a bar chart.
2. Drag Month → Columns.
3. Drag Births → Rows.
4. Sort ascending.

### Expected Output

Lowest birth month appears first.

---

# Exercise 7: Dual Country Comparison 📊

## Business Question

> Which country has higher overall birth rates?

### Steps

1. Drag Country → Columns.
2. Drag AVG(Birth Rate per 1000) → Rows.
3. Add color by Country.

### Expected Output

Comparison bar chart.

---

# Exercise 8: Filtering by Country 🎛️

## Task

Focus analysis on a single country.

### Steps

1. Drag Country → Filters.
2. Select:
   - Portugal OR Chile
3. Click OK.
4. Show Filter.

### Activity

Switch between countries and observe trends.
