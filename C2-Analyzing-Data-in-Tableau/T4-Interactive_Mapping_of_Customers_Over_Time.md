# 🗺️ Interactive Mapping of Customer Activity Over Time (Tableau Lesson) — Summary

---

## 🎯 Key Takeaway

Mapping customer activity helps analysts uncover geographic and temporal patterns that are difficult to identify in traditional tables and reports.

By combining maps, visual encodings, and time-based animation, Tableau enables users to explore how customer activity changes across locations and over time.

---

## 🚲 Objectives of the Chapter

This chapter focuses on:

- Mapping customer activity
- Identifying popular bike locations
- Analyzing station usage
- Understanding geographic patterns
- Visualizing activity over time

These analyses help Divvy better understand customer behavior and improve operational decision-making.

---

## 🗺️ Why Mapping Matters

Geographic visualizations can reveal insights that may be hidden in raw data.

### Benefits of Mapping

- Identifies popular stations and routes
- Reveals geographic usage patterns
- Highlights areas with high demand
- Supports operational planning
- Improves resource allocation

### Business Example

By identifying the most frequently used bike stations and routes, Divvy can:

- Allocate bikes more efficiently
- Improve bike availability
- Reduce shortages at busy stations
- Better meet customer demand

## 🎨 Tableau Visualization Features

Several Tableau features are introduced to enhance geographic analysis.

### Color

Color helps:

- Highlight activity levels
- Reveal patterns
- Differentiate locations
- Draw attention to important areas

---

### Size

Size helps:

- Represent volume
- Indicate magnitude
- Compare station activity levels

Larger marks typically represent greater activity.

---

### Mapping Layers

Mapping layers add geographic context and detail.

They help users:

- Understand spatial relationships
- Identify geographic trends
- Improve interpretation of maps

---

### Tooltips

Tooltips provide additional information when users interact with visual elements.

They can display:

- Station names
- Ride counts
- Customer information
- Additional metrics

#### Benefits

- Adds context without cluttering visuals
- Improves user experience
- Supports deeper exploration

---

## 📅 Visualizing Activity Over Time

One of Tableau's most powerful features for time-based analysis is the **Pages Shelf**.

### What Is the Pages Shelf?

The Pages Shelf allows analysts to animate data across time periods.

Instead of viewing a static snapshot, users can watch activity evolve over time.

### Benefits

The Pages Shelf helps users:

- Observe changing activity patterns
- Track customer behavior
- Identify seasonal trends
- Detect growth or decline
- Reveal hidden temporal patterns

This creates a dynamic and engaging analytical experience.
